What We are Known For

Games

At Pat's Place, patrons can enjoy a friendly game of classic billiards. The inviting pool table, offers a laid-back and social atmosphere. Guests can showcase their cue skills while savoring delicious pizza, making it an ideal setting for both casual hangouts and friendly competitions.

Engaging Community

Engaging the community with our restaurant, Pat's Place, is at the heart of our philosophy. We strive to create more than just a dining experience; we aim to build a vibrant community hub where locals can connect, share, and enjoy moments together.

Food

Indulge in our signature pizzas, each a symphony of flavors harmoniously blending fresh, quality ingredients. From the classic Margherita, adorned with the simplicity of tomato, mozzarella, and basil, to the adventurous combinations of our specialty pies, every bite is a journey through taste and texture.

Live Music

Immerse yourself in the rhythm of live music during our special events. From acoustic melodies to upbeat performances, our live music nights showcase a diverse range of local artists, ensuring there's something for every musical taste. It's more than dining; it's a symphony of flavors and sounds.

Theme Nights and Events

Transforming regular evenings into lively and themed events adds an extra layer of excitement to the pizzeria experience at Pat's Place. We believe in creating a dynamic and engaging atmosphere that goes beyond traditional dining.
